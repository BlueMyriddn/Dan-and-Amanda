The Ceremony

We will be married at the Zion Lutheran Church (webpage) in Geneva at 2:30 PM on Sunday, June 20th. We estimate the ceremony will last for just under an hour with a recieving line immediatly following at the church. Zion Lutheran is located down the street from Belhurst Castle (driving directions) at 18 Snell Road, Geneva

From the Thruway
