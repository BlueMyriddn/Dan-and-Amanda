The Stratosphere

This shot is from the top of the Stratosphere a 1150 foot tower at the north end of the strip. Fremont street has a light show called "The Fremont Experience" that was pretty neat but not really worth the taxi ride all the way there. If you do want to head down there, some folks recomended going early and seeing the Downtown Gordie Brown.
