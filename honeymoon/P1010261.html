New York, New York

The New York, New York even had the Brooklyn Bridge - although it wasn't for sale. New York, New York is also where we went to see Zumanity on Friday night - you can see the billbord for it in the background of this picture. After the show we stopped into a very nice Irish Bar - Nine Fine Irishmen. Both the food and atmosphere were great - we recomend stopping in if you are in Vegas.
