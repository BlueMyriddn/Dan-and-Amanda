Just north of Hearst Castle, we came across a sea lion spot. This big fella was a bunch of fun to watch. He would sit there until a good wave came along and then ride the wave a little further in to shore. He eventually managed to haul himself a good 10-15 feet away from the shoreline before he hunkered down and started covering himself with sand. He sure did put on a great display for us!
