For our first night we stayed at the Madonna Inn (http://www.madonnainn.com) which is a custom themed hotel. Each room has its own particular theme with custom items to give it a little bit of flair. One of its biggest claim to fame is the "rock" rooms with rock waterfalls and the like. These were all booked, but we still found a really fun room to stay in. They also have some really fun curved wall rooms, turrets and the like. I recommend checking out the website to see the diversity.
