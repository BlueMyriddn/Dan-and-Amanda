We cruised from Santa Cruz through the Half Moon Bay and Pacifica area to hit San Francisco. It was a little strange being in the city, especially since our GPS unit seemed to take us through the absolute worst part of the city to get to our hotel. The hotel was in a nice spot though, a block off market street. After checking in and paying a crazy valet parking fee, we hit the streets and went up..and down..and up..and down through the San Francisco hills (wow! very steep!) to end up at Fisherman's wharf just before sunset. That is Alcatraz behind Amanda.
